Party 4 Marty

Portland Oregon:

Marty and Kathie were surprised with a trip to Portland, Oregon to see “I Love Lucy Live”. The show was a reenactment of two I Love Lucy episodes with 50s commercials in-between each episode. Sam, Hugh and Jay threw out the welcome mat to highlight the best of Portland. Marty toured the beautiful falls while Kathie and the girls road the trollie to the shopping district. It was a great weekend getaway.

Stanford Trip:

On May 19th, 2015 Marty and several family members travelled to Palo Alto, CA to meet Dr. Fan at Stanford’s Cancer Center. Dr. Fan and her medical team were extremely caring and educated. They evaluated Marty’s treatment program and we walked away with a positive and hopeful outcome. We will continue to pursue this avenue in conjunction with Marty’s local treatment and care.

Family Reunion:

On Memorial Day weekend two carloads of family traveled from Sacramento, CA to the Central Valley for a family reunion at the Ryan Ranch. The Jordan’s, Motta’s, and Simonsen’s were able to experience Matthew and Scott Ryan’s famous BBQ. There was great food and wine, mini horses, horseback riding and an olive tree planted for our beloved Harriet Ryan who passed away this spring. Overall it was a fantastic reunion and the Ryan’s out did themselves.

Mary Poppins:

A car full of family journeyed down to Chico, CA to watch our little cousin, Mia, début as Jane Banks in Mary Poppins. The family grabbed dinner at our local favorite restaurant “The Hacienda” and a sweet treat afterwards. The play was well put together and Mia truly shined. We expect to see her in many more great performances to come! (please include picture - emailed)
