Marty Jordan

Marty was born June 5, 1957 to Martin and Linda Jordan.

He grew up in a large family with one brother, Ron and three sisters, Paula, Maria and Becky. Marty spent most of his childhood running around and having fun with his friends and cousins.

Marty married his high school sweetheart, Kathie, in 1983. They had two children, Chris (27) and Taylor (23) and now enjoy watching their two grandchildren Jamison (3) and Sofia(1) grow up. Marty’s favorite hobbies are fishing, reading and following the SF Giants and Cal Berkley’s women’s softball team.

- With love from his family

What is he up against

Marty was diagnosed with renal cell carcinoma (kidney cancer) in 2007 and had his right kidney removed shortly after. In the past years the cancer has slowly metastasized into the lungs, liver, spine and brain. After realizing the impact the cancer was having on his body he began stronger cancer treatment options such as chemotherapy and radiation.

So far, results have shown the cancer is still acting aggressive. Marty has maintained a positive attitude throughout this process and is starting to open up and talk about his experience with others. He is currently retired and spends lots of time focusing on resting, holistic healing and surrounding himself with family and friends.
